Our Beginnings

Rooted in Nature,
Driven by Science.

Olee was born from a simple belief: nature provides everything we need to heal and nourish our skin. It started with a family recipe for Tea Tree oil and grew into a passion for sustainable, effective skincare.

We reject harsh chemicals and complicated acronyms. Instead, we embrace transparency, purity, and the potent power of botanical extracts like Mint and Tea Tree. Every bottle is a promise of quality and care.

Our Philosophy

What We Stand For

Planet First

We use recyclable packaging and sustainably sourced ingredients to minimize our environmental footprint.

Cruelty Free

We never test on animals. Our products are 100% vegan and free from animal-derived ingredients.

Radically Transparent

We believe you have the right to know exactly what goes on your skin. No hidden fillers, just pure goodness.
